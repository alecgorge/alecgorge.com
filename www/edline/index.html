About

Alec Gorge created this app because he didn't like using Edline from the browser on his iPhone. He felt it was too slow and didn’t help people access the things they wanted most.

Originally this app was built just for Brebeuf Jesuit Preparatory School in Indianapolis, Indiana, as a side project for Alec.

However, it quickly became clear that there was a big demand for a smooth and efficient app allowed people to access Edline. So Alec generalized the app to work with all Edline accounts around the world.

Popularity of the app grew much faster than Alec could keep up with. The app has now been through many revisions, but it is finally in a place where Alec feels that it can remain stable and useful for many years.
