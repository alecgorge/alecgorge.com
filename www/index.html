this is the personal site of Alec Gorge.

Alec is a moderately cool guy. He makes a mean bagel sandwich and tweets from time to time (although rarely about bagels or sandwiches). He likes writing open-source code on GitHub and answering questions on StackOverflow.

He founded Ramblingwood and Blue Paint, LLC. He runs TINITT with Kevin (he's pretty cool too, I guess) and works with a couple other startups. Last summer he worked on software for a watch produced by some fruit company in Cupertino, CA.

Alec creates iOS apps like Adminium, the Unofficial Edline app, PhishOD, Listen to the (Grateful) Dead and a couple not-yet-finished ones. People spent about 4,968,974 minutes last month using the iOS apps and websites he built. He also has a pretty neat résumé if you're in to that kind of thing.

Alec listens to a lot of Phish, Grateful Dead, Lotus, Sara Bareilles and Acoustic Alchemy. You may also find it interesting that he plays piano and enjoys cooking. Or you might not. It is hard to say.
